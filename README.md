# Progress Tracker

Welcome to Progress Tracker! This is a straightforward, no-nonsense application designed to help you organize your day and keep track of your tasks without feeling overwhelmed.

Whether you are planning your next big project or just trying to remember to get enough sleep, this tool is built to keep your focus exactly where it needs to be.

## What You Can Do

- **Capture Tasks Quickly:** Type out whatever is on your mind and add it instantly to your list.
- **Set Your Priorities:** Not all tasks are created equal, so you can assign a priority level (High, Medium, or Low) to each item to ensure you know what to tackle first.
- **Check Things Off:** There is nothing quite as satisfying as ticking a box. Use the simple round checkboxes to mark tasks as completed as you work through your day.
- **Filter Your View:** If your list is getting too cluttered, use the built-in dropdown to filter your view and only look at "Pending" tasks, "Completed" tasks, or "All" of them at once.
